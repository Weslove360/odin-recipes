Pad See Ew

Description

Pad See Ew is a savory, stir-fried dish. Noodles, broccoli, chicken, and a little bit of egg are covered in a sauce that consists of soy sauce, oyster sauce, vinegar and sugar. This dish name is translated to "Fried with Soy Sauce".

Pad See Ew can be found in any Thai restaraunt in the USA and is often considered a street food that can be found in the various markets in the country with roots tracing back to China.

Ingredients

  • 4 Stems of Broccoli
  • 5 Oz of Chicken
  • 3 tbsp peanut oil
  • 2 Cloves of Garlic
  • 1 Large Egg
  • 2 tbsp dark soy sauce
  • 2 tbsp oyster sauce
  • 2 tsp soy sauc
  • 2 tsp white vinegar
  • 2 tsp sugar
  • 2 tbsp water
  • 7 Oz of dried rice noodles

Steps

  1. Heat 1 tbsp of oil in very large work over high.
  2. Add garlic then chicken, cook until chicken is white.
  3. Add broccoli, cook until chicken is cooked through.
  4. Push everything over and scramble one degg in the pan
  5. Remove ingredients from the wok and set aside.
  6. Mix the soy sauce, vinegar, water, and sugar.
  7. Soak Noodles for 15 minutes
  8. Add remaining oil to the wok.
  9. Once hot, add the sauce and noodles, carmelise eddges of noodles
  10. 2 Add chicken back in and toss.
  11. Enjoy!
