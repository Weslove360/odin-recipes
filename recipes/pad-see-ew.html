Pad See Ew

Description

Pad See Ew is a savory, stir-fried dish. Noodles, broccoli, chicken, and a little bit of egg are covered in a sauce that consists soy sauce, oyster sauce, vinegar and sugar. This dish name is translated to "Fried with Soy Sauce".

Pad See Ew can be found in any Thai restaraunt in the USA and is often considered a street food that can be found in the various markets in the country with roots tracing back to China.
